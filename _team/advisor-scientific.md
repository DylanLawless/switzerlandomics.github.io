## Scientific advisor

The advisor is engaged pre-incorporation and will be formally introduced at a later stage.

This advisor provides senior scientific oversight across genomic methodology, interpretation logic, and evidence standards. The role ensures alignment with established scientific practice while scrutinising assumptions, limitations, and claims made by the system.
